Pronunciation Lesson

Pronunciation Guide

In this lesson, we will practice the pronunciation of various words. Listen to the audio and practice repeating the words aloud.

1. Word: Appreciate

Pronunciation: /əˈpriː.ʃi.eɪt/

Example Sentence: I really appreciate your help with the project.

2. Word: Phenomenon

Pronunciation: /fəˈnɒm.ɪ.nən/

Example Sentence: The Northern Lights are a natural phenomenon.

3. Word: Exaggerate

Pronunciation: /ɪɡˈzædʒ.ə.reɪt/

Example Sentence: She tends to exaggerate her stories to make them more interesting.

Exercise 1: Pronunciation Practice

Exercise 2: Fill in the Blanks

Complete the sentences with the correct pronunciation of the word provided:

  • 1. She was trying to __________ her excitement for the surprise party. (exaggerate)
  • 2. The __________ of the new policy will be seen over time. (phenomenon)
  • 3. I want to __________ how grateful I am for your support. (appreciate)

Answers:

  • 1 - exaggerate
  • 2 - phenomenon
  • 3 - appreciate
